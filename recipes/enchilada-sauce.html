Enchilada Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of flour
  • 3 tbs of chili powder (McCormic)
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 4 1/2 cups of water

Instructions

  1. Very lightly brown 1/2 cup flour in pan
  2. Next, take pan off fire
  3. Then, add 1 teaspoon of salt
  4. Stir it
  5. Then, add 4 1/2 cups of water
  6. Next, Put back on stove on medium heat stirring frequently to a light boil until it thickens a little
