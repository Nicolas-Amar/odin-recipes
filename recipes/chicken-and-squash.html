Chicken and Squash

Description

Chicken and Squash Soup is a perfect meal for cold days, or if you are sick

Ingredients

  • 1 package of boneless skinless chicken
  • 1 tsp of cumino seeds
  • 1 large piece of garlic
  • 1 tbs of tomato sauce
  • 1 tbs of salt
  • garlic powder

Instructions

  1. Trim fat off the chicken thighs
  2. Put salt and garlic powder on both sides
  3. Cook in pan with lid on for 25-30 min
  4. Add water if needed to keep chicken from burning
  5. Once chicken is done, drain the fat and add water to cover the chicken
  6. Add cumino & garlic mixture (grind in mocaiete) and salt and tomato sauce
  7. Add cut up squash and zucchini
  8. Cover and cook on medium high for about 15-20 minutes
