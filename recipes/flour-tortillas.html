Flour Tortillas

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup crisco
  • 1 cup warm water

Instructions

  1. Put all DRY ingredients in a mixing bowl and mix well with a spoon
  2. Add crisco and mix well with hands
  3. Add the warm water and form it into a dough
  4. Cover it with a towel and Let it sit for 20 minutes
  5. Then lightly flour a large cutting board & make small balls of dough
  6. Roll the small dough balls with lightly floured rolling pin.
  7. Cook on the comal
